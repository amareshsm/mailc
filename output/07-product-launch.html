FAQ

Does mailc work in the browser?

Yes! mailc ships ESM and IIFE browser bundles. Use it from a CDN, in a bundler, or server-side with Node.js.

 

What email clients are supported?

All of them. Gmail, Outlook (all versions), Apple Mail, Yahoo, AOL, Samsung Mail, and 50+ more. We test against caniemail.com data.
